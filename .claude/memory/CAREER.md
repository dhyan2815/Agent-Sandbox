## 14. Company Research Framework

Before applying anywhere, research the company using this structured prompt. Paste into Perplexity AI or Claude.

**Quick Research Prompt (v1 — Basic):**
```
Research the company: [Company Name]
Company Website: [URL]
LinkedIn: [LinkedIn URL]

Output:
1. Company Snapshot (industry, product, customers, business model, size, HQ)
2. How the Company Operates (teams, hiring focus areas)
3. Compensation (internship stipend, entry-level salary)
4. Employee Reviews (Glassdoor, AmbitionBox — patterns only)
5. Red Flags & Green Flags
6. Role Suitability (fit score /10, APPLY / CAUTION / AVOID)
```

**Advanced Research Prompt (v2):** Covers 12 sections including tech stack, financial health, leadership transparency, hiring process intelligence. Use for companies you're seriously targeting.

**Key questions to answer before applying:**
- Is this a product company or service company? (Service companies in Gujarat rarely hire AI/ML freshers)
- Is the role remote-friendly?
- What's the stipend range (target ₹15K–25K minimum)?
- Any bond period > 6 months? (Red flag)
- Glassdoor rating + recent reviews?
